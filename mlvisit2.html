About this Event

Join 10 Media Lab students to learn more about their work and the work of their different research groups. After a short welcome, we will break out into small group conversations where participants can ask questions and share ideas.
Each conversation will be hosted by current MIT Media Lab students, who will talk about their research, the philosophy of their groups, and what goes on at the Lab in general. The event will be hosted on the Unhangout platform, a new way of conducting large, online conversations that was developed at the Media Lab. To prepare for the event, have a look at the How-To Unhangout instructions, https://unhangout.media.mit.edu/how-to-unhangout/ and make sure your computer fulfills the basic requirements. Sign up for reminders about the event and get ready to Unhangout!
